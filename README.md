# 🚀 Career Orchestrator: The "High-Grit" Job Search Engine

**Stop draining your energy on manual resume tailoring. Let 4 specialized agents bridge the gap between your unique background and the job you want.**

---

### 📌 The Mission: Why I Built This
Applying for jobs as a non-native English speaker is exhausting. Between fighting with a dictionary and trying to figure out how to explain a career pivot, it’s easy to lose momentum. 

I designed this platform to:
* **Save Your Energy:** No more staring at a blank page; the agents generate a "first draft" of your resume and cover letter in seconds.
* **Fix "Industry Ghosts":** It automatically scrubs out irrelevant industry terms (like "medication" or "pharma") when you're applying for electronics or eCommerce roles.
* **Bridge the Skills Gap:** It uses your **actual quantitative wins** and **academic honors** as proof that you can master new tools (like PIM or Jira) faster than anyone else.

---

### 🧠 How It Works: Your 4-Layer Personal Team
Instead of a generic AI, this system uses 4 specific layers to handle the "hiring filter" while ensuring your resume stays truthful.

| Layer | Responsibility | Why it saves you time |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Strategy Architect** | **Gap-to-Resource Mapping** | Finds exactly what’s missing and gives you a 7-day plan to learn it. |
| **2. ATS Architect** | **Technical Foundation Pivot** | Turns your past work into professional, "action-first" bullets without weird labels. |
| **3. Human Narrator** | **Industry-Specific Guardrails** | Writes a cover letter that sounds like a person, not a robot, using your unique "Grit" stories. |
| **4. Integrity Guardian** | **Level-Gating Simulation** | Drills you on interview questions that actually match the role's seniority. |

---

### 🛠️ Advanced Engineering Features
* **Binary Ingestion Layer:** Supports binary file streams (**PDF/DOCX**) for automated resume parsing.
* **Non-Hallucination Guardrails:** Prompt engineering that strictly distinguishes between **Verifiable Achievements** and **Learning Goals**.
* **Dual-Track Optimization:** Simultaneously optimizes for **Machine Algorithms** (ATS) and **Human Empathy** (Hiring Manager).
* **Voice-First Practice:** Integrated **Voice Ingestion** and **TTS Synthesis** to practice your English and technical answers simultaneously.
